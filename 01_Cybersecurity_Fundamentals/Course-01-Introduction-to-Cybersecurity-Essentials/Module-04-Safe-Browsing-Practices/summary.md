In this module, I learned how to browse the internet safely and avoid common online risks. I understood how the application ecosystem works and how different components like websites, plugins, and extensions can introduce security vulnerabilities.

I explored the risks of public browsing and how unsafe networks can expose sensitive information. I also learned how browser extensions and toolbars can become potential entry points for attacks if not managed properly.

Through the hands-on lab, I practiced managing browser security and privacy settings, which helped me understand how to control my online footprint.

I also learned about VPNs and how they help protect privacy while browsing. Overall, this module improved my awareness of safe browsing habits and how to protect myself from web-based threats.
