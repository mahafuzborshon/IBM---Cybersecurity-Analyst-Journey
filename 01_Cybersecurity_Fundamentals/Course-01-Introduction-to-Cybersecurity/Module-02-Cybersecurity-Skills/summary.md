# Module 02: Cybersecurity Skills

In this module, I explored the core skills required to build a career in cybersecurity. I learned that having strong IT fundamentals is essential before diving deeper into security concepts. Understanding systems, networks, and how technology works gives me a solid base to identify and solve security issues.

I also realized that cybersecurity-specific technical skills are what make someone effective in this field. These include areas like threat detection, security tools, and understanding vulnerabilities. Listening to expert viewpoints helped me see how these skills are used in real-world scenarios.

Another important takeaway for me was the role of soft skills. Communication, problem-solving, and critical thinking are just as important as technical knowledge. Cybersecurity is not only about tools but also about how I analyze situations and communicate risks.

Finally, I learned how different skills map to job roles in cybersecurity. This gave me a clearer picture of which skills I need to focus on depending on the career path I choose. Overall, this module helped me understand what I need to build to become job-ready in cybersecurity.
