# Module 01: Cybersecurity Careers - Summary

At this stage, I started understanding how the cybersecurity field is structured and why it is growing so rapidly.

I learned that cybersecurity is not just one job role, but a wide range of career paths starting from entry-level positions to advanced leadership roles.

I explored how beginners can enter the field and how professionals grow into roles like security analysts, engineers, and architects.

This module gave me a clear idea of real-world cybersecurity careers and helped me understand which path I might follow.
