In this module, I learned how identity and access control play a critical role in cybersecurity. I understood how systems verify users through authentication and control their permissions through authorization. This helped me see how access is managed securely inside organizations.

I also explored concepts like multi-factor authentication and single sign-on, which improve both security and user experience. Learning about modern approaches like passkeys gave me insight into how authentication is evolving.

Another important part was physical security. I realized that cybersecurity is not only about digital protection, but also about securing physical environments like devices and infrastructure.

Overall, this module helped me understand how both digital identity management and physical controls work together to protect systems from unauthorized access.
