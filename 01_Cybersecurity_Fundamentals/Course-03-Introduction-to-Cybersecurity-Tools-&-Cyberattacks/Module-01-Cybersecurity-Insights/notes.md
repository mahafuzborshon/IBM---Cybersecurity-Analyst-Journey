- Cybersecurity evolved through real-world events and incidents
- WarGames movie increased public awareness about hacking risks
- 9/11 led to stronger national cybersecurity and infrastructure protection
- COVID-19 increased cyberattacks due to remote work environments

- Cybersecurity history shows how threats adapt over time
- Organizations improve security after major incidents

- Critical thinking is essential in cybersecurity
- Helps analyze threats, risks, and possible solutions
- Involves questioning assumptions and evaluating evidence

- Critical thinking model includes:
  - Understanding the problem
  - Analyzing information
  - Evaluating options
  - Making decisions

- Key skills in cybersecurity thinking:
  - Analytical thinking
  - Problem-solving
  - Attention to detail
  - Decision-making
  - Adaptability

- Practical activities help apply thinking skills in real scenarios

- Continuous learning and awareness are important in cybersecurity
