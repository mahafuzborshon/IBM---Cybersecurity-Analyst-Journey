In this module, I learned about different types of cybersecurity threats and the people behind them. I explored how attackers have different motivations, such as financial gain, political goals, or just curiosity. This helped me understand that not all cyberattacks come from the same mindset.

I also gained a clear understanding of malware and ransomware, including how they work and how they can damage systems. Learning prevention techniques made me realize how important it is to stay proactive rather than reactive in cybersecurity.

Another important part was social engineering. I understood how attackers manipulate human behavior instead of systems, which made me more aware of phishing and similar attacks.

Overall, this module helped me see cybersecurity from both technical and human perspectives, which is very important for identifying and preventing real-world threats.
