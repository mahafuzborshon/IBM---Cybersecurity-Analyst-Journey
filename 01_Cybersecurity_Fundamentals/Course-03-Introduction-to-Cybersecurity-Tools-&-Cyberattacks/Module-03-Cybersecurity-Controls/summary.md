In this module, I explored different types of internet attacks and how systems can be protected against them. I learned about common attack techniques like packet sniffing, IP spoofing, denial-of-service, and injection attacks, which helped me understand how attackers exploit network and application vulnerabilities.

I also gained knowledge about cybersecurity controls and how they are used to protect systems, networks, and applications. I understood that security is not just about tools, but also about proper design and planning.

This module also introduced me to important areas like vulnerability management and incident response. It gave me a clear idea of how organizations detect, respond, and recover from cyber incidents.

Overall, this module helped me connect attacks with defense strategies, which is very important for building a strong cybersecurity mindset.
